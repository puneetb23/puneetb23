Hostel

Hostel is provided for GIRLS and BOYS inside the campus.
Students are provided with adequate facilities to make them feel at home.
Their stay in the hostel enables them to imbibe a different lifestyle and involve them in activities Including Yoga, Meditation, Sports, Music etc, suiting individuals taste and preferences.
GPN HOSTEL IS EQUIPPED WITH :
  • 24 hours electricity and water supply
  • Green refreshing surroundings to relax.
  • First aid facilities and visit of doctors on call
  • Airy and well furnished rooms
  • Hygienic, tasty and nourishing foods to inmates.



    • Hostel Rules :

    Hostel fee is to be paid in advance during the starting of each session. Hostel fee will not be refundable in case of expulsion from hostel by the management. Shifting of furniture from one room to another is not allowed. No interchange of room is permitted without the written permission of the hostel warden. During class timings the students are not allowed to stay in the hostel rooms.. Morning assembly and exercises are compulsory for all hostlers, however relaxation/exemption may be given in case of physical disability on case-to-case basis. Hostel Fee Candidates who are seeking hostel accommodation will have to pay a sum of Rs. -------/- per annum which includes boarding and mess charges.

    Library

    Every bonafide student can obtain the Institute Library membership. The students are issued borrower’s cards for issue and return of books and journals. The borrowers’ cards are issued to every student on applying through a prescribed form which is to be recommended by Head of Department/Department In charge . The norms for the issuance of books/journal are as follows. Limit of Books: (For all Student) 2 Books for 15 days. Issue Hours for Library Books: (Monday to Saturday) 10.30 A.M. to 1.00 P.M. & 2.00 P.M. to 4.30. P.M Overdue Charges: Rs. 1.00 per day per book If any reader is found involved in stealing/destroying or damaging any type of reading materials, he may suspension of Library membership for six months. BOOK BANK RULES Book Bank's membership form and Borrower's card may be obtained from the Library. The form is to be submitted to Library after the recommendation of Head of Department / Department In charge. Maximum four books are issued at a time under the Book Bank scheme. Loss of Books In case of loss or damage caused to the issued books, the student will be required cost of the books
